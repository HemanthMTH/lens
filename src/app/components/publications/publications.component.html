PrivacyLens is an automated framework that collects privacy policies of smart devices from different sources continuously. After collection, PrivacyLens applies a suit of AI analysis algorithms to extract insights including information about their readability or the most important privacy concepts included. PrivacyLens collects and analyzes also past privacy policies for the last few years to give a timeline and explore changes due to, for instance, data regulations. By providing valuable insights and comparisons, PrivacyLens aims to enhance the usability and effectiveness of these policies for end-users ultimately empowering users to make better-informed decisions when purchasing and using smart devices.
