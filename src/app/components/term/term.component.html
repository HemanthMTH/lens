Readability Analysis

Our framework captures the readability of a policy by extracting the following features using NLP, along with Ambiguity:

  • 1. Coherence score
  • 2. Entropy
  • 3. Frequency of Unique Words
  • 4. Reading Level
  • 5. Reading Time
  • 6. Frequency of Imprecise Words
  • 7. Frequency of Connective Words
  • 8. Grammatical Errors

Privacy Analysis

Our framework dynamically identifies the instances of certain privacy attributes within each Privacy Policy.

  • 1. Do Not Track
  • 2. Data Security
  • 3. First Party Collection
  • 4. Third Party Collection
  • 5. OPT-OUT
  • 6. User Choice
  • 7. Data
  • 8. Legislation
  • 9. Access/Edit/Delete
  • 10. Policy Change
