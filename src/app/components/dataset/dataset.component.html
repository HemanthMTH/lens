Our dataset presents an interesting exploration into the privacy policies of various websites, with a particular focus on the types of devices they cater to. The device categories range from general to miscellaneous, representing the diversity in the digital ecosystem. Interestingly, a significant number of websites do not explicitly mention devices in their privacy policies. This highlights an area of potential improvement, as transparency about device-specific data collection and use is an essential aspect of user trust and regulatory compliance. The dataset also captures various other privacy-related keywords, including 'First Party Collection', 'Third Party Collection', 'OPT-OUT', 'User Choice', and more.
