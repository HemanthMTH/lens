The PrivacyLens dataset is continuously evolving. As of now, it contains 1200 privacy policies extracted for 5198 smart devices from 32 categories. The following graph shows the distribution of privacy policies based on different categories of smart devices.

PrivacyLens collects and analyzes privacy policies from smart devices from manufacturers all around the world. This following map provides a geographical representation of privacy policies of different manufacturers across the globe. The colors represent the proportion of such policies for each country, expressed as a percentage of the total count. Hover over a country to see the specific percentage. For instance, a higher percentage indicates that a greater proportion of policies in that country explicitly mention smart devices.

Not all the privacy policies available online explicitly mention the smart device itself (sometimes they just apply to the website of the manufacturer or the app associated with the device).
