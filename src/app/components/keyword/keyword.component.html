Keyword Analysis

This section details the keyword analysis that was conducted in the study to gain insights into the privacy policies of various smart devices. We conducted a keyword analysis of multiple smart device privacy policies and grouped the keywords into ten distinct groups to identify trends and patterns. We then analyzed the frequency of these groups across all the devices studied to gain a comprehensive understanding of the current state of privacy policies in the smart device industry. Then extracted relevant keywords from each policy and obtained the Maximum, Minimum, and Average values for each group across all devices studied. This allowed them to gain valuable insights into the privacy practices of different smart devices.
